About The Penguin Company

Our mission

The Penguin Company's mission is to raise awareness about penguin conservation and research while giving everyday people a chance to interact with these amazing birds!

Our history

The Penguin Company began in 2015, when we, a team of marine biologists and wildlife preserve rangers, rescued a colony of penguins from an unprecedented environmental crisis. Off the coast of a remote island near the Antarctic Peninsula, a massive glacial collapse disrupted the habitat of an endangered group of emperor penguins. The sudden influx of melting ice caused severe flooding, threatening the colony's survival.

Partnering with local conservation organizations, we relocated the penguins to a specialized habitat in a nearby wildlife sanctuary. Realizing the ongoing need to protect these penguins, we founded The Penguin Company as a unique initiative to combine conservation efforts with public education and interaction.

We began offering guided experiences where people could interact with the rescued penguins, all under the careful supervision of wildlife experts. By allowing people to rent time with penguins in ethical, controlled environments, the company aims to raise awareness about penguin conservation while also funding research and habitat preservation.
